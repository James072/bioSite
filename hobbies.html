James' Hobbies

James' mother started him playing the piano at the young age of 12 years old. Unlike other kids who were made to do things by their parents, James enjoyed playing the piano. He admits that he slacked-off from playing in his early twenties and picking it back up after purchasing his own piano when he was 27. Although James could play gospel music, most of his time was spent practicing classical pieces by Beethoven, Bach and Mozart. His favorite piece to play was "Moonlight Sonata" by Ludwig Von
Beethoven.

One of James' favorite activities was working on cars. His knowledge of cars saved him a lot of money in auto repairs. He spent a lot of time during the 80's trying to rebuild his Chevy Super Bee, which he eventually let go.

*For more on self-auto repair:
10 Car Problems You Can Fix Yourself

One of the things that James was well known for was his cooking skill. Whether it was firing up his homemade grill (made out of an old oil barrel) to fill hungry stomachs at the church picnic, or in the kitchen baking his famous cookies and peanut clusters to fill holiday orders, James put his heart and soul into every dish. He also enjoyed cooking for large gatherings.
